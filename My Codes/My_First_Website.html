Dirk Nowitzki




Bio

Dirk Nowitzki from Wurzburg Germany is a professional basketball player for the Dallas Mavs. He has led the Mavericks to 15 NBA Playoffs and 2 NBA Finals and 1 Championship.

Nowitzki is the highest-scoring foreign-born player in NBA history and the sixth player to achieve over 30,000 Points.He is the first Maverick voted onto an All-NBA Team and holds several all-time Mavericks franchise records.

Career Highlights
  • 13x Time ALL STAR
  • 2011 NBA Champion
  • 2007 NBA MVP
  • 6th All Time NBA Scoring Leader
